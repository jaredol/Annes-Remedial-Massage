Anne Horee - Massage Therapist

Anne has over ten years experience as a Massage Therapist. She specializes in Remedial Massage, Deep Tissue and Relaxation Massage. Previously, Anne has worked for the Hilton Hotel in Phuket, Bangkok and Maldives. She came to Australia in 2007 to work as a massage therapist in Brisbane before moving to Melbourne 4 years ago. Anne has worked for Uspa Immersion in Brighton for 3 years before starting her own home based and mobile business.

Treatments

Remedial Massage $90/hour

Remedial massage uses specific techniques to stretch and lengthen connective tissue and muscles, reduces pain and discomfort, bringing wellness and sense of relaxation and mobility to client. Remedial Massage is to speed venous blood return form the extremities (arms and legs). Remedial Massage shortens recovery time from muscular strain by flushing the issue of lactic acid. It improves circulation and stretches the ligaments and tendons, keeping them supple and young.

Deep Tissue Massage $90/hour

Deep-tissue massage is a specific type of massage therapy that concentrates on the deep layers of muscle and fascia in the body. By using deep finger pressure and slow, firm strokes, deep tissue massage is used to treat a variety of physical ailments to reduce chronic pain and improve blood pressure.

Relaxation Massage $80/hour

A gentle flowing massage to soothe and quietly release tensions, perfect for those that need help switching off the brain and a completely restful experience. It help to clam the nervous system and promotes a sense of relaxation and well being, reduces tension and anxiety, improves blood circulation which delivers oxygen and nutrients to the cells.
